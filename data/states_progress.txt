# STATE SEED DATA GUIDELINES
# Updated: 2026-04-28

## METAL DETECTING ALLOWED ✅
- BLM land (Bureau of Land Management)
- USFS land (US Forest Service - National Forests, NOT Wilderness)
- State Parks (verify per state, many require permits)
- State Wildlife Management Areas (WMAs)
- Army Corps of Engineers lake/reservoir land
- County/City public parks (verify locally)
- Public beaches (below high tide line)
- Historic railroad grades on public land
- Ghost towns on BLM/USFS land

## METAL DETECTING PROHIBITED ❌
- ALL National Park Service (NPS) sites
- National Historic Sites (NHS)
- National Historical Parks (NHP)
- National Monuments
- National Battlefields (if NPS managed)
- Wilderness Areas
- Many State Historic Sites (verify each)
- Archaeological protected sites

## DEDUPLICATION REQUIRED
Before adding any site to a state seed file, check these files for duplicates:
- All state seed files (seed_*.json)
- data/battles_seed.json
- data/frrandp_ghost_towns.json
- data/historic_camps.json
- data/historic_trail_landmarks.json
- data/stagecoach_stops.json
- data/abandoned_churches.json
- data/abandoned_fairgrounds.json
- data/historic_brothels.json
- All mine seed files (seed_mines_*.json)

If site name exists OR coordinates are within ~0.01 degrees, find different site.

## VERIFICATION CHECKLIST
Each site must:
1. ✅ Be on public land where detecting is ALLOWED
2. ✅ Be accessible right now (no gates/fees/special access)
3. ✅ Be pre-1940 (older preferred)
4. ✅ Have exact coordinates from official source
5. ✅ NOT duplicate any existing seed data
