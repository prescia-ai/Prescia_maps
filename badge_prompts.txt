========================================================
PRESCIA MAPS — AI IMAGE PROMPTS FOR ALL ACHIEVEMENT BADGES
========================================================

All badges follow the same base specification:
- Diamond shape (square rotated 45 degrees)
- Flat vector illustration style
- Bold, vibrant colors with 2-3 color maximum per badge
- Simple geometric shapes with clean edges
- No gradients — solid color blocks only
- Modern, game-like aesthetic similar to Strava achievement badges
- High resolution (at least 1024x1024px)
- PNG format with transparent background
- No text on badge
- Icon centered and prominent, may break diamond border boundaries for visual pop
